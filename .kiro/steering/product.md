# Product: Uppal Kalan Street Food Guide

## Purpose
An AI-powered local guide that helps residents and visitors discover
authentic street food experiences specific to Uppal Kalan, Telangana.
The tool understands local food culture, budget constraints, and
neighborhood specifics.

## Local Context for Kiro

### Geography
- Located in Hyderabad's eastern zone, Medchal-Malkajgiri district
- Proximity to IKEA Hyderabad, major retail parks and IT hubs
- Major roads: Uppal-IKEA road, Main Uppal Road, LB Nagar-Uppal road
- Local landmarks: Uppal market, Surya Cinema, Uppal Stadium, Ramanthapur
- Well-connected via MMTS train (Uppal station) and metro connectivity

### Food Culture & Local Specialties
- **Telangana cuisine dominance**: Biryani, Haleem, Mirchi ka Salan, Qubani ka Meetha
- **Street food popular**: Samosas, Dosas, Chaat, Pani Puri, Mirchi Bajji
- **Local breakfast**: Idli-Sambar, Upma, Pesarattu, Dosa varieties
- **Evening snacks**: Bajjis, Bonda, Vada, Tea with local biscuits
- **Budget-friendly options**: Most street food ₹20-100, meals ₹80-200
- **Spice preference**: Medium to high spice levels preferred locally
- **Vegetarian options**: Abundant due to diverse population

### Local Dining Patterns
- **Morning rush (7-10 AM)**: Office workers want quick breakfast - Idli, Dosa, Upma
- **Lunch crowd (12-2 PM)**: IT employees, shoppers - Biryani, meals, tiffins
- **Evening social dining (6-9 PM)**: Families, friends - Chaat, snacks, dinner
- **Weekend patterns**: Longer dining times, family outings, trying new places
- **Festival seasons**: Special sweets, traditional foods gain popularity

### Notable Eating Areas & Vendors
#### Uppal Market Area
- **Street food hub**: Multiple vendors selling fresh snacks
- **Specialties**: Mirchi Bajji, Samosas, Fresh fruit chaat
- **Timing**: 6 AM - 10 PM daily
- **Crowd**: Local residents, market shoppers
- **Price range**: ₹20-80 per item

#### Main Uppal Road
- **Mixed dining**: Street vendors + small restaurants
- **Popular items**: Dosas, Biryani, Chinese fast food
- **Peak hours**: 8-10 AM, 1-2 PM, 7-9 PM
- **Accessibility**: Easy parking, bus stop nearby

#### Near IKEA & Retail Parks
- **Modern food courts**: Branded outlets + local vendors
- **Target audience**: Shoppers, families, young professionals
- **Cuisine variety**: North Indian, South Indian, Chinese, Continental
- **Price range**: ₹100-300 per person

#### Ramanthapur Junction
- **Traditional eateries**: Established local restaurants
- **Authentic Telangana food**: Traditional recipes, family-run businesses
- **Specialties**: Authentic Hyderabadi Biryani, local sweets

### Target Users
1. **IT professionals**: Working in nearby Gachibowli, Hitech City - need quick, tasty meals
2. **IKEA visitors**: Shoppers planning meals before/after shopping
3. **Local residents**: Seeking variety in daily dining, weekend exploration
4. **Students**: Budget-conscious, looking for filling, affordable meals
5. **Families**: Weekend dining, special occasion meals, kid-friendly options
6. **Tourists**: Visitors wanting authentic Telangana food experience

### Seasonal & Cultural Considerations
- **Monsoon (June-September)**: Hot snacks like Bajjis, Pakodas more popular
- **Winter (November-February)**: Outdoor dining preferred, street food peak season
- **Summer (March-May)**: Cold drinks, ice creams, lighter meals preferred
- **Ramadan**: Haleem becomes extremely popular, special Iftar items
- **Festival seasons**: Diwali sweets, Ganesh Chaturthi modaks, regional specialties

### Budget Profiles
- **Student budget**: ₹50-100 per meal
- **Working professional**: ₹100-200 per meal
- **Family dining**: ₹300-600 for 4 people
- **Special occasions**: ₹500-1000 for family

### Transportation & Accessibility
- **MMTS connectivity**: Uppal station connects to Secunderabad, Hyderabad
- **Bus routes**: Multiple TSRTC routes connecting to all major areas
- **Auto availability**: Good auto-rickshaw connectivity
- **Parking**: Street parking available, some restaurants have dedicated parking

## Key Features to Build
1. **Smart food recommendations** based on budget, time, and preferences
2. **Local restaurant/vendor directory** with authentic reviews
3. **Telangana cuisine education** - explain dishes, ingredients, cultural significance
4. **Real-time busy level estimates** based on time and day
5. **Budget-based filtering** - find great food within user's price range
6. **Dietary preference support** - vegetarian, vegan, Jain food options
7. **Peak hours & wait time estimates** to help users plan visits
8. **Delivery vs dine-in recommendations** based on location and preferences

## Business Objectives
1. **Become the go-to guide** for authentic Uppal Kalan food experiences
2. **Support local street vendors & businesses** by increasing visibility
3. **Reduce decision fatigue** for diners with too many choices
4. **Document and preserve** local food culture and traditions
5. **Bridge the gap** between tourists and authentic local experiences

## Success Metrics
1. **50+ local vendors/restaurants** documented with accurate information
2. **Positive user feedback** on recommendation accuracy and local authenticity
3. **Reduced lookup time** - users find suitable food options in under 2 minutes
4. **Community engagement** - locals contributing reviews and new discoveries
5. **Cultural preservation** - documenting traditional recipes and food stories
